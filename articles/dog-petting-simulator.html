Dog Petting Simulator

20/12/2021

Dog Petting Simulator Demo was a project I worked on a few years ago with a couple of students from The Animation Workshop (TAW), including my Sister, Kristina. As part of their education they had to do a self study. Their group wanted to try and make a video game and therefore approached me as Kristina and I shared a passion for video games and she knew I had programmed a little as part of my education as IT-Supporter.

Momo and Eddie

It sounded like a fun project so I said yes, asked my boss for vacation from work and spend the next three weeks in Viborg living at my Sisters working on Dog Petting Simulator. Our team consisted of three animators and myself as the programmer.
We decided to make the game in Unity and after some long evenings and lots of coffee we got the demo ready so they could deliver it for their exam. We released it on Itch.io and after a few weeks it got some traction. The game got downloaded by YouTubers who made playthroughs of the game, including IGN. The Guardian also did an article on the popular Twitter user @CanYouPetTheDog where Dog Petting Simulator was showcased. The game is free to get and can be found on itch.io by clicking here.
